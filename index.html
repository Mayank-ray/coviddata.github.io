What is COVID-19 (Corona-Virus)

Coronaviruses are a large family of viruses that are known to cause illness ranging from the common cold to more severe diseases such as Middle East Respiratory Syndrome (MERS) and Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS).

A novel coronavirus (CoV) is a new strain of coronavirus that has not been previously identified in humans.

6 Steps Prevention Against Coronavirus

Wash your hands regularly for 20 seconds, wit soap and water or alcohol-based hand rub

People should avoid touching their eyes, nose, and mouth with their hands, especially if they are unwashed.

People should take care to avoid coming into close contact with others especially those who are older, unwell, or have symptoms of the virus.

Avoid crowded places and try not to take part in public gathering

Always wear mask when going out meeting with people, maintain 6 feet distance

Don't go out, try working from home. Stay home stay safe.
